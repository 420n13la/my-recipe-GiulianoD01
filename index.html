Pizzelle

Introduction

This italian cookie is thin and crunchy. it is very fast and esay to make and can be used in lots of things.

Ingredients

  • 7 eggs
  • 1.5 cups sugar
  • 1 tbs of vanilla
  • .5 cups of oil
  • 4.5 cups of flour
  • 2 tbs of baking powder

Steps

  1. heat up pizzelle iron
  2. mix the eggs, sugar, vanilla, and oil until homogenous.
  3. add dry ingredients slowly
